SnapMeta Privacy Policy

SnapMeta does not collect, store, or share personal data directly.

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information (such as device identifiers) for advertising purposes in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

SnapMeta does not store or process photo data outside the user's device. All EXIF metadata is read locally and is not transmitted to any server.

For more information about Google AdMob data practices, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy
