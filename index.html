SnapMeta Privacy Policy

SnapMeta does not collect, store, or share personal data directly.

This app reads EXIF metadata from photos selected by the user. All processing is performed locally on the user's device. No photo data or metadata is transmitted to any server.

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and process certain information such as device identifiers, usage data, and advertising identifiers for advertising and analytics purposes.

This app may collect and share data through third-party services such as Google AdMob for advertising purposes.

Data collection and processing by Google AdMob are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
